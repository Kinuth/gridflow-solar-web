Overview

GridFlow Solar (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (for example, when you contact us using the contact form or email). This may include your name, email address, and message. We also automatically collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and pages visited through server logs and analytics tools.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve the website, remember preferences, and analyze site usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

Third‑Party Vendors and Advertising

We use third‑party services (including analytics and advertising providers) to help operate the site and show relevant content. Third‑party vendors, such as Google, may place their own cookies on your device and use them to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites. These ads may be personalized and are served according to the third party's policies. For Google’s advertising and privacy options, see Google Ads Settings and the Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, provide and maintain the website, understand usage patterns, improve our content and services, and for security and fraud prevention.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, email delivery). These providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide those services to us.

Your Choices

You can opt out of cookies by adjusting your browser settings and opt out of targeted advertising by visiting the advertising choices pages provided by ad networks (for example, Google’s Ads Settings). To request access, correction or deletion of your personal data collected via our contact form, email us at info@gridflowsolar.com.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@gridflowsolar.com.
